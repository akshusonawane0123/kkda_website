Course Description

In our music lessons, students will delve into the rich world of guitar, flute, and ukulele, each offering a unique set of skills and experiences. With the guitar, students will discover the art of chord progressions, strumming patterns, and expressive techniques like fingerpicking, empowering them to play a wide range of musical styles. In flute lessons, students will embark on a journey of breath control, finger dexterity, and nuanced articulation, as they learn to craft beautiful melodies and explore the emotive depth of this elegant instrument. Meanwhile, ukulele lessons will introduce students to the joy of simple yet captivating chords, rhythmic strumming, and the art of fingerstyle playing, fostering a love for music that is both accessible and enchanting. Whether strumming chords on the guitar, weaving melodies on the flute, or plucking strings on the ukulele, students will find inspiration and fulfillment in the unique offerings of each instrument, guided by experienced instructors dedicated to nurturing their musical growth and creativity.

Flute Course Curriculum

Learn how to play flute instrument

Introduction to the Flute:
Understanding the anatomy of the flute Proper posture and hand positioning Introduction to embouchure (lip placement) and breath control techniques
Basic Techniques:
Learning the first octave of notes on the flute Mastering fingerings for basic scales (e.g., C major) Developing tone quality and dynamics through simple exercises
Articulation and Phrasing:
Exploring different articulation techniques (e.g., tonguing, legato) Understanding phrasing and expression in flute playing
